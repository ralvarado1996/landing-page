Who We Are

AgroDignity is a Honduran social enterprise transforming agricultural data into dignified income for smallholder farmers. We believe farmers already generate valuable digital assets every day — yet they are rarely compensated for them.

×

Our Mission

To formalize and monetize agricultural digital assets to close the rural income gap. We verify data once and license it across multiple markets, ensuring each use generates royalties for farmers.

×

Our Vision

A world where agricultural data is recognized as farmer-owned property, where traceability generates income, and where digital sovereignty strengthens rural independence.

×

The Project

We verify agricultural data under international standards, register it as a Digital Asset Identifier (DAI), license it across regulatory, carbon, AI, and finance markets, and distribute 90% of royalties directly to farmers.
