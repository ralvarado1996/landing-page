Who We Are

AgroDignity is a Honduran social enterprise transforming agricultural data into dignified income for smallholder farmers. We believe farmers already generate valuable digital assets every day — yet they are rarely compensated for them.

Founded by professionals with experience in agribusiness, global food law, and international development institutions, we design digital infrastructure that converts verified agricultural data into royalty-generating assets.

Our Mission

To formalize and monetize agricultural digital assets in order to close the rural income gap. We verify data once and license it across multiple markets — regulatory compliance, carbon, research, artificial intelligence, and rural finance — ensuring every use generates royalties for farmers.

Our Vision

A world where agricultural data is recognized as farmer-owned property, where traceability generates income rather than compliance costs, and where digital sovereignty strengthens rural economic independence.

How the Project Works

1. Verification
Farmers submit GPS polygons, production records, and documentation. We verify compliance under international standards such as EUDR and carbon protocols.

2. Digital Asset Registration
Each verified dataset receives a Digital Asset Identifier (DAI) within a Cooperative Digital Registry.

3. Multi-Market Licensing
Data is licensed across multiple markets:

  • European regulatory compliance
  • Carbon credit systems
  • Agricultural research institutions
  • AI training datasets
  • Rural insurance and finance

4. Royalty Distribution
90% of access fees are distributed directly to farmers as royalties. 10% supports a community Dignity Fund. AgroDignity does not retain farmer royalties.

Impact

By activating existing digital records as income-generating assets, AgroDignity creates new financial pathways without increasing farmer debt or dependency. It is not subsidy — it is market correction.
